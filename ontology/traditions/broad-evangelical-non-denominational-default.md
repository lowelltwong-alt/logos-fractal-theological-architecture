# Broad Evangelical Non-Denominational Default

Author: Lowell T. Wong  
Status: draft  
Node type: tradition_stance  
Canonical ID: tradition.broad_evangelical.non_denom_default

## Summary

A starter tradition profile for users who identify as Christian, often Bible-first, usually Protestant in instinct, but without strong formal denominational commitments.

## Why this profile matters

Many lay users will not enter the system through formal theological language. They often arrive with a real Christian moral instinct, but without a settled map of church history, sacramental theology, or authority structures.

This profile helps the system avoid forcing precision too early.

## Common characteristics
- strong emphasis on Scripture
- strong concern for personal faithfulness
- strong concern for morality and truth
- often cautious about deception and sexual confusion
- mixed depth on tradition, church authority, and theological history
- often practical and issue-driven rather than institution-driven

## Likely AI emphasis areas
- truth versus deception
- dignity and personhood
- sexual ethics and relational confusion
- spiritual formation and dependence
- stewardship and responsibility

## Risks in discernment
- underestimating the role of tradition and historical theology
- relying on intuition without doctrinal mapping
- collapsing all issues into obvious-sin versus not-obvious-sin
- lacking categories for structural power and formation effects

## Best system use
Use this profile when the user:
- identifies as non-denominational
- has broad Protestant assumptions
- wants plain-language guidance first
- has not settled many secondary doctrinal issues
