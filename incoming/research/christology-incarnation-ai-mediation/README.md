# Christology, Incarnation, and AI Mediation Research Seed

## Purpose

This packet stages a vertical slice about how Christology and the incarnation should constrain AI systems that mediate speech, counsel, representation, or human presence.

The core issue is that AI may summarize, route, draft, and assist, but should not falsely present itself as embodied, accountable, pastoral, sacramental, ecclesial, or morally final.
