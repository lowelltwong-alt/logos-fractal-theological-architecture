# Conservative Theologian Anchor Map

## Selection criteria

A conservative evangelical source anchor should be evaluated by:

- recognition among conservative churches;
- doctrinal orthodoxy;
- biblical authority posture;
- usefulness for doctrine/concept architecture;
- public-domain or licensing feasibility;
- denominational scope and controversy profile;
- suitability for source-profile treatment.

- J. I. Packer
- John Stott

- Kevin DeYoung
