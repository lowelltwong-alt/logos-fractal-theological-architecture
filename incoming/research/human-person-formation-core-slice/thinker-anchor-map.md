# Thinker Anchor Map

## Augustine

Primary function in this slice:

- ordered love;
- interiority and will;
- fallenness as pride, self-love, and damaged willing;
- grace as necessary reordering;
- non-naive institutional realism.

Candidate concept links:

- `concept.ordered_love`
- `concept.fallenness` or `concept.sin`
- `concept.grace`
- `concept.personhood`
- `concept.human_agency`

## Athanasius

Primary function in this slice:

- incarnation as restoration and renewal of humanity;
- Christ as the true Image renewing humanity made after the image;
- anti-reductionist account of body, creation, and redemption.

Candidate concept links:

- `concept.imago_dei`
- `concept.personhood`
- `concept.grace`
- `concept.hope`

## Dallas Willard

Primary function in this slice:

- formation as reordering of the whole person;
- heart/will as executive center;
- apprenticeship, practices, attention, and responsibility;
- systems form persons through repeated practice.

Candidate concept links:

- `concept.formation`
- `concept.human_agency`
- `concept.personhood`
- `concept.ordered_love`

## Henri Nouwen

Primary function in this slice:

- servant leadership;
- vulnerability and presence;
- resistance to relevance, popularity, and dominating power;
- formation of leaders under humility rather than control.

Candidate concept links:

- `concept.formation`
- `concept.suffering_and_lament`
- `concept.personhood`
- `concept.ordered_love`

## Tim Keller

Primary function in this slice:

- vocation/work as service and meaning;
- public faithfulness;
- work under Christian wisdom rather than self-interest alone;
- institution-facing translation of theological anthropology.

Candidate concept links:

- `concept.vocation`
- `concept.work`
- `concept.human_agency`
- `concept.formation`

## Calvin / Luther later

Use later rather than overloading this slice.

Potential roles:

- Calvin: knowledge of God/self, image, original sin, governance seriousness, providence.
- Luther: faith and works, ordinary calling, neighbor-serving vocation, anti-self-justifying works.

Do not create full Calvin/Luther buildouts in this branch unless explicitly requested.
