# Concept Nodes

## Purpose

This folder holds concept nodes for the concordance graph.

Concept nodes are reusable conceptual objects that connect scripture references, doctrines, themes, and interpretations without collapsing into one verse or one topic label.

## Core rule

A concept node should be more precise than a broad topic and less tradition-bound than a full doctrine node.

## What belongs here

- reusable concepts such as kingdom of God, covenant, ordered love, image of God
- links to relevant verses, passages, doctrines, and interpretation objects
- provenance and review state where conceptual claims matter

## Summary principle

Concept nodes are one of the main ways the concordance becomes an ontology seed rather than only an index.
