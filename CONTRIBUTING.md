# Contributing

Start with [`AI_FRONT_DOOR.md`](AI_FRONT_DOOR.md).

The short rule:

- If your work is unreviewed research, put it under `incoming/research/`.
- If your work changes doctrine, concept definitions, graph relationships, trust zones, or governance rules, expect steward review.
- If AI helped draft the work, say so and do not mark it canonical.
- Do not add private, sensitive, pastoral, personnel, client, or confidential information.
- Do not overwrite stable doctrine files wholesale.
- Do not invent node types, relationship types, or lifecycle states casually.

Good contributions are small, traceable, source-aware, and easy to review.

Bad contributions are broad, unsourced, polemical, overconfident, or hard to separate into source, claim, inference, and application.
