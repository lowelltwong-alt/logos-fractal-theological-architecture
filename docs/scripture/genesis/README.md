# Genesis

## Why Genesis matters to this project

Genesis is one of the most important scripture-book nodes in the repository because it anchors the project's thinking about creation, human identity, vocation, authority, fall, disorder, labor, and the moral structure of reality.

For this theological architecture, Genesis matters not only as background, but as a first-order source for:
- creation as good and ordered
- the human person as image-bearing
- authority and stewardship under God
- the fall as moral and relational disorder
- labor, toil, and creaturely responsibility

## Why this book belongs early in the scripture layer

Genesis should be one of the first scripture books built because it connects directly to:
- Augustine
- Aquinas
- anthropology doctrine
- image-of-God themes
- stewardship and vocation themes
- AI and governance questions about delegated authority, human dignity, and creaturely limits

- personhood
- work
- power
- order
- fallenness
- governance under moral limit
