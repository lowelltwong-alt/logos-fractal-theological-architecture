# Trust Zones (Phase 1)

Trust zones define authority boundaries.

## canonical
Authoritative, human-approved content.

## tradition-scoped
Authoritative within a defined tradition or profile.

## proposed
Pending human review.

## inferred
Machine-derived output; never canonical by itself.

## deprecated
Historical but preserved.

## learning-sidecar
AI suggestions and feedback signals only.

## One-way rule
Lower-trust zones may reference higher-trust zones.
Higher-trust zones may not depend on lower-trust zones.

## Retrieval default
Canonical only unless explicitly expanded.
