# Sin

## Purpose

This node preserves sin as a reusable cross-branch concept in the Logos repository.

## Why this node matters

Sin explains why creaturely goods become distorted in use, why responsibility is corrupted by rebellion and pride, and why information or capability alone cannot repair moral disorder.

## Early grounding focus

The first grounding focus for this concept should be:
- Genesis 3
- fall theme work
- anthropology under distortion
- later governance and AI application work on power, misuse, and overreach
