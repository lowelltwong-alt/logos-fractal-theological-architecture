# Imago Dei

## Purpose

This node preserves the image of God as a reusable cross-branch concept.

## Why this node matters

The image of God is central for:
- theological anthropology
- dignity
- personhood
- stewardship
- labor and responsibility
- institutional and AI-era reasoning about what may or may not be delegated

## Early grounding focus

The first grounding focus for this concept should be:
- Genesis 1:26-28
- anthropology doctrine work
- lexical notes on `tselem` and `demut`
- later comparative interpretation and doctrinal development
