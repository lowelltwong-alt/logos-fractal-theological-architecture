# Fall

## Purpose

This node preserves the fall as a recurring biblical theme that explains disorder, rebellion, distortion, and the corruption of creaturely responsibility.

## Why this node matters

The fall is necessary for the repository because theology of dignity without theology of distortion becomes naive. It helps explain why persons, institutions, tools, and governance systems become disordered even when created goods remain real goods.

## Early grounding focus

The first grounding focus for this theme should be:
- Genesis 3
- anthropology doctrine work
- vocation and stewardship under distortion
- later governance and AI application work about misuse, pride, and overreach
